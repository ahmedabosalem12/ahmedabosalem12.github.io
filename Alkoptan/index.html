About us

Al Koptan for Shipping is an Egyptian marine service provider established in 2003. And provides full integrated services to the Shipping, Logistics & Maritime markets for both local and foreign companies operating in its branches in Suez and Alexandria. Our offices covering the main ports in Egypt such as Damietta, Port Said East, Port Said, Sokhna, Alexandria and Dekheila port. We achieve the goals of our company whatever the assignments are. We are responsible for ensuring that our customers are completely satisfied with our service which means getting the shipment from the supplier to the destination on time, within cost, in minimum time and in perfect condition. A great Progress was achieved and proofing the ability to work under pressure during the last years as we were considered as one of too small number of companies were able to proceed and maintain the business in Sokhna Port during the highly tough situations we met in marine service
