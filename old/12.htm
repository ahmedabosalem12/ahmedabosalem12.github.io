Suez Canal Transit Steps:

*On arrival unit inspection by suez canal servioer

*Supply mooring rope and shore labours if not available on the unit in order

  to assist suez canal tug during transit suez canal  until clear from channel

*Towing certificate require

*Register on suez canal office in order to arrange transit

*Unit will be transit by suez canal imposed 2 tug for towing unit according S.C rulls.

*This operation take maximum 2-4 days  from arrival to transit channel.
