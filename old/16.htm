Highly customable cost efficient service at every port of call in Egypt in order to attend all requirements of vessels, owners or operators.

Husbandry requirements and needs are a significant part of a vessel's activity in any voyage. Therefore, special care should be made to assure smooth operation while reducing expenses to minimum.
Alradwan, with its unique personal relationships, provides you with highly experienced services to attend all requirements of vessels, owners or  operators,  and  ensure  a  quick  and  cost efficient  service  at every port of  call at Egypt

Our service include:
          
• - Special arrangements for discharging slops and sludge's, gray water , sewage (Environmentally certified)
• - Supply of fresh water, bunker fuel, chemicals and lubricants.
• - Fresh provision and boned stores
• - Smooth delivery of cash to master.
• - Underwater works including cleaning and Inspections.
• - Express spare parts handling.
• - Special repairs on board and ashore.
• - Booking dry / floating dock for annual and special survey
• - Work shop assistant
• - Technical supply
• - Gases , paints , life rafts inspection
• - Independed surveys 
• - Crew changes, transportation and accommodation in all ports.
• - Accompaniment to medical services to crew, or any other assistant vessel may require.
